Individual Project Site Plan

Little Explorers Nursery School

Jardín Maternal “Los Pequeños Exploradores” – Salta, Argentina

1. Site Name

Site Name: Little Explorers Nursery

Reason: The name is warm, positive, and easy to remember. It conveys curiosity, learning through play, and adventure — ideal for a nursery school..

2. Site Purpose

The website provides clear, precise, and attractive information for parents in Salta who are looking for a reliable nursery. It presents the nursery’s mission, educational programs, services, daily routines, facilities, and easy enrollment options. The goal is to help families quickly find the information they need and build trust in the center.

3. Scenarios

  • As a mother searching for daycare in Salta, what ages does the nursery accept and what are the opening hours?
  • What educational activities and programs does the nursery offer to support my child’s early development?
  • How can I schedule a visit, see the facilities, and complete the enrollment process?

4. Color Scheme

A soft, joyful pastel palette that feels welcoming and child-friendly.

#a8d5ba – Soft Mint Green
Used for: Header background, main accents, buttons, and navigation.

#f8f1f9 – Soft Blush Pink
Used for: Main background and light content areas.

#e07a5f – Warm Coral
Used for: Headings, call-to-action buttons, and important links.

#2c3e50 – Deep Slate Blue
Used for: Body text and dark accents (ensures good contrast).

5. Typography

  • Heading Font: Comic Neue
    Used for: All main headings (h1, h2, h3) – gives a playful and friendly feeling suitable for a nursery.
  • Body Font: Segoe UI (system sans-serif fallback)
    Used for: Paragraphs, lists, and all body text – clean, readable, and professional for parents.
